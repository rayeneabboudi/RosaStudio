Frequently Asked
Questions

How long do your preserved flowers last? +

Our preserved arrangements are designed to maintain their beauty for 1 to 3 years with minimal care. They are real flowers treated with a special preservation liquid.

What is your delivery area? +

We currently offer express shipping nationwide and same-day local delivery for orders placed before noon.

Do I need to water the flowers? +

No, please do not water them. Preserved flowers do best in dry environments away from direct sunlight and high humidity.
